ABOUT US

“Stri” offers traditional and indo - western apparel for women and other accessories. Our brand follows the motto of our respected Prime Minister Narendra Modi's "vocal for local" or localization aim. Our products are produced by fine craftsmen and designers who craft exquisite styles and unique designs providing a wide range of options for all women customers. Thus, we use pure cotton collected from one of India's finest artisans and manufacturers.


We understand the rich culture and traditions that our rural communities are steeped in and we translate this heritage into products by pairing traditional art with contemporary designs and modern processes. As a brand, Stri Handicrafts constitutes ladies’ wear and other accessories. Stri products use mirror work, patchwork and embroidery created as a vibrant expression of the rural way of life, their rituals and their legends.
